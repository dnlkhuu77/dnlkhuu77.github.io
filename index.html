About Me

Hello. My name is Daniel Khuu. I'm currently a software engineer associate at Fidelity Investments. I am a Stony Brook alumni who graduated in Fall 2017 with a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Writing.

I began programming when a friend introduced it to me freshmen year in college at a time when I didn't know what to do in life. It was hard at first (and it's still hard), but I hope to see a future of developing software.

I find that the world of programming is an expanding field that should be only taken seriously if you work hard and have perseverance. My goal is to stick through the frustrations and challenges of coding and be able to enjoy what I do.
